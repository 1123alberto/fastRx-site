# FastRx Website Repositioning
## Marketing Phase 1 — Build Curiosity

This task is NOT a redesign.

It is a complete marketing repositioning of the existing FastRx landing page while preserving the current visual identity, typography, spacing, color palette and overall premium appearance.

The goal is to transform the website from:

"A website describing prescription software"

into

"A website that immediately convinces Greek clinicians that FastRx will save them time every day."

---

## Product Identity

FastRx is NOT:

- an Electronic Health Record
- a Practice Management System
- an AI diagnosis tool
- an AI treatment recommendation system
- a replacement for IDIKA
- a replacement for the official e-Prescription platform

FastRx IS:

A modern clinical workflow application that dramatically simplifies electronic prescription creation and diagnostic examination referrals while remaining fully aligned with the official Greek e-Prescription workflow.

Think of it as:

"The same official workflow.
Far fewer clicks.
Far less friction."

Everything on the homepage must reinforce this positioning.

---

## Overall Design Direction

Do NOT redesign the site.

Keep:

- typography
- colors
- whitespace
- layout
- cards
- contact form
- premium minimalist appearance

Only improve:

- messaging
- hierarchy
- storytelling
- user flow
- calls-to-action

The result should feel closer to Apple's product pages or Linear.app rather than a corporate software brochure.

---

# Primary Marketing Objective

The visitor should leave with one thought:

"FastRx will save me time every single day."

NOT

"FastRx is another prescribing application."

---

# Hero Section

Rewrite the hero.

The current wording is technically correct but emotionally weak.

The headline should focus on:

- reducing unnecessary work
- reducing clicks
- simplifying prescribing
- giving clinicians more time

Do NOT overpromise.

Do NOT use marketing clichés.

Avoid words like:

- revolutionary
- disruptive
- AI-powered
- next generation

The tone should be calm, confident and professional.

---

The CTA button should no longer be:

"Επικοινωνία"

Replace it with an action that matches the website goal, such as:

- Δήλωση ενδιαφέροντος
- Ζητήστε πρώιμη πρόσβαση
- Συμμετοχή στην Early Access

The button should smoothly scroll to the contact form.

---

# Phone Mockup

The phone is now one of the strongest visual assets.

Improve it without changing the design language.

Increase its visual importance.

If technically possible:

Create a slow slideshow cycling through real application screens every 6–8 seconds:

- Patient Search
- ICD-10 Selection
- Medication Selection
- Review
- Confirmation

Transitions should be elegant and subtle.

No flashy animations.

---

# Workflow Section

The five workflow cards are excellent.

Keep them.

Improve only:

- copy
- visual hierarchy

If appropriate, visually connect the cards to reinforce that they represent one continuous workflow.

---

# Trust Section

Create a completely new section before the contact form.

Purpose:

Explain WHY FastRx exists.

Suggested theme:

Designed by a clinician for everyday clinical practice.

The copy should communicate that FastRx was created by someone who understands the daily frustration of unnecessary clicks in the official workflow.

Avoid talking about company history.

Focus on solving a real clinical problem.

---

# Status Section

Reword the current development notice.

Current wording feels defensive.

Instead communicate:

FastRx is actively being refined with clinicians before public release.

Maintain transparency.

Do not imply the application is incomplete or unreliable.

---

# Contact Section

Keep the existing form.

Rewrite the surrounding copy.

Instead of asking users to "contact us",

invite them to become early participants.

Explain what they receive:

- Early access
- Updates
- Opportunity to influence development
- Future testing opportunities

The section should feel like joining something valuable rather than sending an email.

---

# Footer

Minor improvements only.

Maintain minimalism.

---

# Copywriting Style

The writing should feel like:

Apple

Linear

Notion

Stripe

Professional.

Minimal.

Clear.

Every sentence should earn its place.

No filler.

No exaggerated claims.

---

# SEO

Preserve important keywords naturally:

- ηλεκτρονική συνταγογράφηση
- παραπεμπτικά
- ICD-10
- συνταγογράφηση
- ιατροί
- επαγγελματίες υγείας

Do not keyword-stuff.

---

# Critical Constraint

Never position FastRx as replacing IDIKA.

FastRx complements and streamlines the official workflow.

The official submission process remains authoritative.

---

# Deliverables

Implement:

- improved hero
- improved CTA
- rewritten section copy
- new trust section
- improved development section
- improved contact section
- smoother storytelling
- optional phone slideshow (if feasible)

without changing the premium visual design already established.

The visitor journey should naturally progress through:

Problem

↓

Solution

↓

Workflow

↓

Trust

↓

Invitation to join

↓

Contact

The finished homepage should feel less like a software brochure and more like the introduction to a product that clinicians will genuinely look forward to using.
